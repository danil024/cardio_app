# Privacy Policy

**Cardio HR Monitor** — Last updated: March 13, 2026

## Data We Collect

Cardio HR Monitor collects **heart rate data** from your Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) heart rate sensor during workout sessions. This data is processed and stored **locally on your device only**.

## How Data Is Used

- Heart rate readings are displayed in real time on your screen.
- Session data (timestamps and BPM values) is saved to local CSV and JSON files in your device storage for your personal review and export.
- No data is transmitted to any server, cloud service, or third party.

## Permissions

| Permission | Purpose |
|---|---|
| Bluetooth / BLE | Connect to heart rate sensors |
| Location (Android) | Required by Android OS for BLE scanning (no GPS data is collected) |
| Storage | Save workout session logs to device |

## Third-Party Services

This application does **not** use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Data Retention

All data is stored on your device. You can delete individual sessions or all data from within the app's History screen. Uninstalling the app removes all stored data.

## Children's Privacy

This application is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.
